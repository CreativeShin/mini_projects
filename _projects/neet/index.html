Who are we?

This is a support group intended to help struggling NEETs who wish to reintegrate with society to whatever extent on their own reasonable terms. There are quite a few NEET groups on the internet, but many of them are full of toxic people who would rather stay depressed and make no changes to their lifestyle. Becoming a productive member of society doesn't mean you have to completely give up your interests. It is good however, to improve yourself, expand your horizons, and let go of bitterness. We want to create an encouraging supportive community.
